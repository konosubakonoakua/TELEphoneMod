TELEphone is a mod including a new Item "TELEphone" 
which gives you ability to teleport to anywhere
pressing right mouse button when openning full-screen map.

Caution: player will have a confused debuff lasting for about 3 seconds after teleportion.
Ingredients: MagicMirror、Teleporter*99、TriFold

全屏地图右键传送，传完会有几秒的困惑 Debuff，效果可以被饰品免疫。
制作：魔镜、传送机*99、三折地图
